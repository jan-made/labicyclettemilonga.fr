FR: Tu as été accepté.e !

L'équipe du marathon de la Bicyclette a le plaisir de te confirmer ta participation à la deuxième édition de notre marathon !

15-17 mai 2026

TRAC L'Ecole, Toulouse

Afin de finaliser ton inscription, merci de respecter ces deux étapes :

1) Un virement de 90€. Ce paiement ne comprend ni l'hébergement, ni de « vrais » repas. Tu trouveras nos coordonnées bancaires ci-dessous. • Tableau pour un hébergement chez un.e toulousain.e

EN: You have been accepted!

The organizing team of La Bicyclette Marathon is very pleased to inform you that you are in for the second edition of our marathon!

15-17 May 2026

TRAC L'Ecole, Toulouse

In order to confirm your participation, we need you to follow these two very important steps:

1) Proceed to the payment of 90€ before April 1st, 2026. Since we have a long waiting list, we will offer the spot to someone else if we haven't received a payment after this deadline. Bank details below. Please remember that this amount does not include « real » meals or accommodation.

2) Send us a picture of you (as a child or as an adult) on a bike or next to a bike — only if you were not at the first edition. The picture will be projected during the marathon. It won't be used for any other purposes.

In order to make your journey easier, here are links for:

• Free accommodation sheet (locals willing to hosts)
• Car-sharing sheet

Do not hesitate to contact us if you're encountering payment issues or if you need any further information. We are looking forward to welcoming you!
